Week 2b: You are as good as your game

Wednesday, Jun 17, 2020| Tags: outdoor, sports, tennis

Day 10 of tennis

Day 10 of formal tennis on June 17, 2020. Following learnings from today:

It was just an average play today. Got a chance to play rallies with a senior coach and realized that one is as good as one’s game.

  1. Loss of focus and technique: The alertness and awareness converts into caution causing unforced errors and miscalculations. The placement judgement dips and thus the quality of shots deteriorates.

  2. Reduced stamina: If the game is not going right, the mind diverts into negative areas like tiredness, feeling fatigue and loss of interest and confidence. It hurts the overall game more.

  3. Consistency: The consistency in each shot to follow through and technique derive your worth in the game. Need to be consistent with every game and point, ensuring the rally continues and prove yourself that you are there to improve each day.

Nevertheless, will show up tomorrow. Plan to buy a balanced racquet with a 100 sq. in. head.

On the court - you are as good as your game!
